# 60 Second Demo Script

## 0-10 seconds

"This is BYVA, a loan EMI voice agent for BFSI support. It handles inbound support and outbound EMI reminders."

## 10-25 seconds

Pick `Nisha Rao` and ask: "Why was I charged a late fee?"

"The agent identifies late-fee intent, checks the customer account using a backend tool, retrieves the late-fee policy, and answers with a grounded explanation."

## 25-40 seconds

Ask: "I already paid and want a refund."

"Now the agent detects a payment dispute, creates a support ticket, and escalates because refund decisions need human review."

## 40-50 seconds

Click `Outbound reminder`.

"The outbound flow reminds the customer that EMI is due and offers pay now, callback, or support."

## 50-60 seconds

Show analytics.

"After each call, the dashboard captures intent, sentiment, resolution status, next action, escalation reason, transcript, tools, and policy evidence."
