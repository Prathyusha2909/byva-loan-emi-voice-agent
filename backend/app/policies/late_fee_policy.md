# Late Fee Policy

Late fees apply when the EMI is not received by the end of the grace period.
For standard retail loans, the grace period is three calendar days after the due date.
After the grace period ends, a late fee of INR 450 or 2 percent of the overdue EMI,
whichever is lower, may be charged.

The assistant may explain why a late fee was added, confirm the due date, and create
a dispute ticket if the customer says payment was already completed. The assistant
must not waive a fee directly.
